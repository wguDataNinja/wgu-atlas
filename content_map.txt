  Sections
----------------------------------------------------------------------

  Section 1: What is WGU Atlas?
    WGU Atlas is an independent reference built from WGU's public academic catalog.
    It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by Western Governors University.
    Atlas helps students explore WGU degree options, compare related degrees, and
    understand how degrees and courses have changed over time when those changes
    are relevant to what they're viewing.

  Section 2: What it covers
    • Current degrees across WGU's four colleges
    • Course catalog with history going back to 2017
    • Degree comparisons for related degrees
    • Retired degrees still visible for reference
    • College background and earlier names over time

  Section 3: Source and independence
    All data is derived from WGU's publicly available course catalog, scraped and
    parsed from 108 catalog editions spanning January 2017 through March 2026.
    No internal WGU systems or private data were accessed.
    Atlas is a community project. Not a substitute for official WGU advising.

  Section 4: How history and resources are used
    Where a degree or college has changed names or structure over time, Atlas surfaces
    that history where relevant — earlier names on detail pages. History is secondary
    context, not the primary lens.

  More detail links:
    Methods & Caveats: → /methods  — how data was collected, validated, and how to interpret it
    Data: → /data  — download the canonical datasets behind Atlas
    Timeline: → /timeline  — major WGU catalog events from 2017 to 2026
